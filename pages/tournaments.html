Tournaments

National Ballin' Tournament (12/3/22-12/5/22)

This tournament is our signature tournament of the year, giving players around the nation the opportunity to compete with one another to earn the "National Baller" title. There will be a men's tournament, women's tournament, co-ed tournament, and youth tournament. All members are invited to play and all people are invited to spectate. The tournament will be held at our San Antonio, TX facility. Sign-ups close on 11/20/22, so be sure to sign up prior!

New Year New Tournament (1/10/23)

What better way to kick off the new year than with a new tournament? This tournament is a women's tournament and will be held at our Phoenix, AZ location. Whether you are currently a Pickle Ballin' member or not, you are welcome to play or to spectate! Sign-ups will be available until the tournament begins. We hope to see you there!

The Classic Tournament (3/15/23-3/17/23)

That's right, another one of our classic tournaments! This tournament will include men's and women's play. Youth are permitted to play in this tournament if they are teamed up with an adult. The Classic Tournament will be held in our Provo, UT facility. Sign-ups are only available through 3/1/23, so make sure you don't miss it!

80's Disco Tournament (4/22/23)

At this tournament we want to know who has the best hits from the 80's. This tournament will be for men and women who were born in the 80's and think that they have what it takes to take home the title. The tournament will be held at our Miami, FL location and sign-ups will be available until the tournament begins. Come ready to bust a move!
